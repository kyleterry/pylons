.. _glossary:

Glossary
========

.. glossary::

    Pylons
        A Python-based WSGI oriented web framework.
    
    WSGI
        The `WSGI Specification <http://www.python.org/dev/peps/pep-0333/>`_,
        also commonly referred to as PEP 333 and described by :pep:`333`.
    
    tmpl_context
        The tmpl_context is available in the :mod:`pylons` module, and refers
        to the template context. Objects attached to it are available in the
        template namespace as either ``tmpl_context`` or ``c`` for 
        convenience.
